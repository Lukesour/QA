## Weekly Decision Rules

- Keep: article variants that win for two consecutive weeks on `read completion + DM rate + consult booking`.
- Rework: topics with `read completion` below threshold for two weeks.
- Pause: topics with high reads but weak DM/form performance.
- Scale: topics with stable DM->consult->deal conversion.

## Owner Fill Priority (Hard Requirement)

- Priority 1: `Read Completion Rate`, `DMs`, `Form Submits`.
- Priority 2: `Consult Bookings`.
- Priority 3: `Deals`, `Deal Amount`.
- Weekly SLA: every Monday 12:00 (Asia/Shanghai) fill all Priority 1 fields for last full week.

## Two-Week Seed Sprint KPI Gate

- Scope: `A01/A02/A03/A09/A17/A24/A25/A29`
- Must-have gate before publish amplification:
  - `Read Completion Rate` present
  - `DMs` present
  - `Form Submits` present
  - matching case proof bundle uploaded
