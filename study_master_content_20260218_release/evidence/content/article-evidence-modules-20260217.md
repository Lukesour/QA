## UK (A01-A08)

### A01
- not_applicable_group: 已获海外硕士且短期无转岗计划人群。
- official_sources: [UK Student visa money](https://www.gov.uk/student-visa/money), [UK IHS](https://www.gov.uk/healthcare-immigration-application/how-much-pay), [Exeter deadlines](https://www.exeter.ac.uk/study/postgraduate/applying/applicationdeadlines/).
- key_checkpoint: 伦敦 £1,529/月、非伦敦 £1,171/月（最多9个月）；学生IHS £776/年。
- case_bundle: `CASE-UK-001`.
- owner_fill_slots: `预算表实算截图 + 执行清单 + 结果对比`.

### A02
- not_applicable_group: 目标纯学术深造（非就业导向）且不追求短期回报的人群。
- official_sources: [UK Graduate visa](https://www.gov.uk/graduate-visa), [UK Student visa money](https://www.gov.uk/student-visa/money).
- key_checkpoint: Graduate Route 本硕 2026-12-31 前通常2年，自 2027-01-01 起18个月；PhD 3年。
- case_bundle: `CASE-UK-002`.
- owner_fill_slots: `适配评分表 + 入学前补强计划`.

### A03
- not_applicable_group: 已完成定校并支付押金的人群。
- official_sources: [Exeter deadlines](https://www.exeter.ac.uk/study/postgraduate/applying/applicationdeadlines/), [Glasgow taught programmes](https://www.gla.ac.uk/postgraduate/taught/).
- key_checkpoint: Exeter 2026/27 国际DDL为 2026-09-11 23:59（UK time）；Glasgow 页面可提取 January/September 入学月。
- case_bundle: `CASE-UK-003`.
- owner_fill_slots: `四维打分表前后对比`.

### A04
- not_applicable_group: 已锁定伦敦并有稳定赞助来源的人群。
- official_sources: [UK Student visa money](https://www.gov.uk/student-visa/money), [Study in the UK cost guide](https://www.ucas.com/money-and-student-life/money/studying-abroad/cost-studying-uk).
- key_checkpoint: 伦敦与非伦敦财力要求有明确差值，预算应按保守版+风险金测算。
- case_bundle: `CASE-UK-004`.
- owner_fill_slots: `城市双方案预算 + 月度现金流`.

### A05
- not_applicable_group: GPA已显著高于目标项目均值且无需补强的人群。
- official_sources: [Exeter deadlines](https://www.exeter.ac.uk/study/postgraduate/applying/applicationdeadlines/), [UK Student visa financial evidence guidance](https://www.gov.uk/guidance/financial-evidence-for-student-and-child-student-route-applicants).
- key_checkpoint: 建议按DDL倒排，优先补“可验证证据”，避免仅靠动机陈述。
- case_bundle: `CASE-UK-005`.
- owner_fill_slots: `补强任务甘特图 + 材料改前改后`.

### A06
- not_applicable_group: 已完成文书定稿并通过院校面向国际学生审稿的人群。
- official_sources: [Exeter deadlines](https://www.exeter.ac.uk/study/postgraduate/applying/applicationdeadlines/), [Glasgow taught programmes](https://www.gla.ac.uk/postgraduate/taught/).
- key_checkpoint: 文书应与项目课程和就业落点一致，避免“泛叙事”。
- case_bundle: `CASE-UK-006`.
- owner_fill_slots: `文书结构图 + 段落重写示例`.

### A07
- not_applicable_group: 仅单一offer且无替代选项的人群。
- official_sources: [UK Graduate visa](https://www.gov.uk/graduate-visa), [UK IHS](https://www.gov.uk/healthcare-immigration-application/how-much-pay), [Exeter deadlines](https://www.exeter.ac.uk/study/postgraduate/applying/applicationdeadlines/).
- key_checkpoint: 终选要把签证窗口、IHS与岗位落点放入同一加权表。
- case_bundle: `CASE-UK-007`.
- owner_fill_slots: `offer加权终选表 + 敏感性测试`.

### A08
- not_applicable_group: 仅计划读博或短期不进入就业市场的人群。
- official_sources: [UK Graduate visa](https://www.gov.uk/graduate-visa), [UK Student visa money](https://www.gov.uk/student-visa/money).
- key_checkpoint: 求职窗口需前置，毕业签证申请需在学生签证到期前完成。
- case_bundle: `CASE-UK-008`.
- owner_fill_slots: `12周求职计划 + 周复盘数据`.

## HK (A09-A16)

### A09
- not_applicable_group: 目标为长期海外移民且不考虑香港就业路径的人群。
- official_sources: [HK ImmD Study](https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/services/visas/study.html), [HK IANG](https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/services/visas/IANG.html).
- key_checkpoint: IANG 为 quota-free/non-sector specific；近期毕业生可获24个月逗留。
- case_bundle: `CASE-HK-001`.
- owner_fill_slots: `港硕适配评分 + 路径对照图`.

### A10
- not_applicable_group: 仅按城市偏好决定，不做项目匹配的人群。
- official_sources: [HKBU timeline](https://gs.hkbu.edu.hk/admission/research-postgraduate-programmes/application/admission-timeline), [EdUHK TPG intake](https://www.apply.eduhk.hk/tpg/full-time).
- key_checkpoint: HKBU AY2026/27 关键DDL为 2025-12-01 与 2026-04-15。
- case_bundle: `CASE-HK-002`.
- owner_fill_slots: `项目筛选矩阵V1/V2 + 最终名单`.

### A11
- not_applicable_group: 已完成全部材料并仅等待提交的人群。
- official_sources: [HKBU timeline](https://gs.hkbu.edu.hk/admission/research-postgraduate-programmes/application/admission-timeline), [HK ImmD Study](https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/services/visas/study.html).
- key_checkpoint: HK 学签材料齐全后常规处理约6周，申请倒排应预留签证缓冲。
- case_bundle: `CASE-HK-003`.
- owner_fill_slots: `12周倒排甘特图 + DDL风险看板`.

### A12
- not_applicable_group: 背景已完全满足录取画像且无需补强的人群。
- official_sources: [HK ImmD Study](https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/services/visas/study.html), [HKBU timeline](https://gs.hkbu.edu.hk/admission/research-postgraduate-programmes/application/admission-timeline).
- key_checkpoint: 学签申请需有录取证明、资金与担保资料，低背景更需前置证据链。
- case_bundle: `CASE-HK-004`.
- owner_fill_slots: `补强任务板 + 结果对比`.

### A13
- not_applicable_group: 已有企业全额赞助且现金流无压力的人群。
- official_sources: [HK ImmD Study](https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/services/visas/study.html), [GovHK e-visa payment](https://www.gov.hk/en/residents/immigration/nonpermanent/onlinepaymentforevisa.htm).
- key_checkpoint: 预算需覆盖签证/住宿/求职期现金流，不只学费。
- case_bundle: `CASE-HK-005`.
- owner_fill_slots: `理想版/保守版预算 + 偏差复盘`.

### A14
- not_applicable_group: 无面试环节项目或已完成面试的人群。
- official_sources: [HKBU timeline](https://gs.hkbu.edu.hk/admission/research-postgraduate-programmes/application/admission-timeline), [HK ImmD Study](https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/services/visas/study.html).
- key_checkpoint: 面试准备应和项目定位、材料叙事保持一致。
- case_bundle: `CASE-HK-006`.
- owner_fill_slots: `题库页 + 模拟面试复盘`.

### A15
- not_applicable_group: 非当季入学且不在准备窗口的人群。
- official_sources: [HK ImmD Study](https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/services/visas/study.html), [HKBU timeline](https://gs.hkbu.edu.hk/admission/research-postgraduate-programmes/application/admission-timeline).
- key_checkpoint: 入学前需并行处理证件、住宿、选课与求职启动。
- case_bundle: `CASE-HK-007`.
- owner_fill_slots: `入学前7件事完成率`.

### A16
- not_applicable_group: 已拿长期雇主担保并完成去向锁定的人群。
- official_sources: [HK IANG](https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/services/visas/IANG.html), [HK ImmD annual report](https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/publications/a_report_2024.html).
- key_checkpoint: IANG 初始逗留通常24个月，后续可按3-3模式续签（符合条件可top-tier延长）。
- case_bundle: `CASE-HK-008`.
- owner_fill_slots: `双路径投递日志 + 终选节点`.

## SG (A17-A23)

### A17
- not_applicable_group: 仅为学历镀金且不考虑就业落地的人群。
- official_sources: [MOM EP eligibility](https://www.mom.gov.sg/passes-and-permits/employment-pass/eligibility), [ICA Student's Pass](https://www.ica.gov.sg/reside/STP/collect).
- key_checkpoint: EP当前门槛包含 S$5,600/金融S$6,200；页面注明2027年起提高至 S$6,000/6,600。
- case_bundle: `CASE-SG-001`.
- owner_fill_slots: `适配评分卡 + 留新/回国双路径图`.

### A18
- not_applicable_group: 已锁定单校单项目且不比较路径的人群。
- official_sources: [NUS SoC application](https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/pg/misc/application/), [NTU CW guide](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/admissions/graduate/cwadmissionguide).
- key_checkpoint: NUS SoC August intake 窗口为 2025-10-01 至 2026-01-31；NTU AY2026-27 含 August/November/January 多窗口。
- case_bundle: `CASE-SG-002`.
- owner_fill_slots: `院校矩阵 + 入学窗口表 + 权重结果`.

### A19
- not_applicable_group: 材料已完成多轮审核且一致性通过的人群。
- official_sources: [NUS SoC application](https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/pg/misc/application/), [NTU CW guide](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/admissions/graduate/cwadmissionguide).
- key_checkpoint: 申请周期短，材料一致性（简历/文书/推荐信）比堆叠素材更关键。
- case_bundle: `CASE-SG-003`.
- owner_fill_slots: `一致性检查表 + 修订记录`.

### A20
- not_applicable_group: 已拿到确认奖学金且覆盖主要成本的人群。
- official_sources: [ICA Student's Pass](https://www.ica.gov.sg/reside/STP/collect), [MOM EP eligibility](https://www.mom.gov.sg/passes-and-permits/employment-pass/eligibility).
- key_checkpoint: STP签发费 S$60、MJV S$30（如适用）；预算底盘不应按“必中奖学金”计算。
- case_bundle: `CASE-SG-004`.
- owner_fill_slots: `无奖学金版/有奖学金版预算`.

### A21
- not_applicable_group: 不计划在新加坡就业的人群。
- official_sources: [MOM EP eligibility](https://www.mom.gov.sg/passes-and-permits/employment-pass/eligibility), [NTU CW guide](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/admissions/graduate/cwadmissionguide).
- key_checkpoint: 求职节奏要和学期节奏并行，避免把投递压缩到学期后段。
- case_bundle: `CASE-SG-005`.
- owner_fill_slots: `12周求职计划 + 反馈率趋势`.

### A22
- not_applicable_group: 目标专业与现有背景高度一致的人群。
- official_sources: [NUS SoC application](https://www.comp.nus.edu.sg/programmes/pg/misc/application/), [NTU CW guide](https://www.ntu.edu.sg/admissions/graduate/cwadmissionguide).
- key_checkpoint: 跨专业应优先提供可验证能力迁移证据，而非仅动机陈述。
- case_bundle: `CASE-SG-006`.
- owner_fill_slots: `迁移叙事改前改后 + 项目证据`.

### A23
- not_applicable_group: 已接受单一路径offer并完成合同签署的人群。
- official_sources: [MOM EP eligibility](https://www.mom.gov.sg/passes-and-permits/employment-pass/eligibility), [ICA Student's Pass](https://www.ica.gov.sg/reside/STP/collect).
- key_checkpoint: 留新可行性受岗位门槛影响，建议并行路径后再收敛。
- case_bundle: `CASE-SG-007`.
- owner_fill_slots: `双路径投递看板 + 终选日志`.

## AU (A24-A30)

### A24
- not_applicable_group: 只做长期科研路线且不关注就业回报的人群。
- official_sources: [Student visa (subclass 500)](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/student-500), [QILT GOS](https://www.qilt.edu.au/surveys/graduate-outcomes-survey-(gos)).
- key_checkpoint: 学签可在课程期间每两周工作48小时；2024 GOS显示就业指标较2023回落。
- case_bundle: `CASE-AU-001`.
- owner_fill_slots: `三笔账模型 + 政策成本对照`.

### A25
- not_applicable_group: 已确定项目并支付学费不可逆转的人群。
- official_sources: [UNSW admissions timeline](https://www.unsw.edu.au/study/international-students/2025-admissions), [QILT GOS](https://www.qilt.edu.au/surveys/graduate-outcomes-survey-(gos)).
- key_checkpoint: 不同学校/term窗口差异大，需用“窗口+岗位+成本”做组合决策。
- case_bundle: `CASE-AU-002`.
- owner_fill_slots: `冲稳保组合矩阵 + 城市权重`.

### A26
- not_applicable_group: 已完成提交并进入审理的人群。
- official_sources: [UNSW admissions timeline](https://www.unsw.edu.au/study/international-students/2025-admissions), [Student visa (subclass 500)](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/student-500).
- key_checkpoint: 申请节奏需按院校term节点倒排，签证材料准备需并行。
- case_bundle: `CASE-AU-003`.
- owner_fill_slots: `12-16周甘特图 + 完成率`.

### A27
- not_applicable_group: 已锁定城市且具备稳定本地住宿资源的人群。
- official_sources: [Study Australia living costs](https://www.studyaustralia.gov.au/en/life-in-australia/living-and-education-costs), [Cost of living calculator](https://costofliving.studyaustralia.gov.au/).
- key_checkpoint: 应按城市做全成本预算（房租、通勤、求职成本）而非只看学费。
- case_bundle: `CASE-AU-004`.
- owner_fill_slots: `城市对照表 + 月度复盘`.

### A28
- not_applicable_group: 仅以学术兴趣选课且不做就业导向的人群。
- official_sources: [QILT GOS](https://www.qilt.edu.au/surveys/graduate-outcomes-survey-(gos)), [Student visa (subclass 500)](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/student-500).
- key_checkpoint: 课程与岗位证据链应闭环，避免“课程好过但求职断层”。
- case_bundle: `CASE-AU-005`.
- owner_fill_slots: `JD-课程映射表V1/V2`.

### A29
- not_applicable_group: 不计划在读期间实习的人群。
- official_sources: [Student visa (subclass 500)](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/student-500), [QILT GOS](https://www.qilt.edu.au/surveys/graduate-outcomes-survey-(gos)).
- key_checkpoint: 在读求职需遵守签证工时条件并以周复盘驱动迭代。
- case_bundle: `CASE-AU-006`.
- owner_fill_slots: `12周SOP + 投递反馈日志`.

### A30
- not_applicable_group: 无备选offer或决策约束极低的人群。
- official_sources: [QILT GOS](https://www.qilt.edu.au/surveys/graduate-outcomes-survey-(gos)), [Student visa (subclass 500)](https://immi.homeaffairs.gov.au/visas/getting-a-visa/visa-listing/student-500), [UNSW admissions timeline](https://www.unsw.edu.au/study/international-students/2025-admissions).
- key_checkpoint: 终选应用“岗位匹配+总成本+风险+时间窗”统一加权，避免单看名气。
- case_bundle: `CASE-AU-007`.
- owner_fill_slots: `加权终选表 + 敏感性测试`.
