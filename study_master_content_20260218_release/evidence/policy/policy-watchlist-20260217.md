## UK

- Student visa financial evidence (official, GOV.UK)
  - London: GBP 1,529/month (up to 9 months)
  - Outside London: GBP 1,171/month (up to 9 months)
  - Funds held for 28 consecutive days, ending within 31 days before applying
  - Source: https://www.gov.uk/student-visa/money
- Student visa application fee: GBP 524 (outside UK and in-UK extension/switch)
  - Source: https://www.gov.uk/student-visa
- Immigration Health Surcharge: students/dependants GBP 776/year
  - Source: https://www.gov.uk/healthcare-immigration-application/how-much-pay
- Graduate visa
  - Before 2027-01-01: usually 2 years (PhD 3 years)
  - On/after 2027-01-01: usually 18 months (PhD 3 years)
  - Source: https://www.gov.uk/graduate-visa

## Hong Kong

- Study visa normal processing time: about 6 weeks
  - Source: https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/services/visas/study.html
- IANG first stay: 24 months
  - Source: https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/services/visas/IANG.html
- Fee update effective 2025-02-26
  - Application fee HKD 600
  - Visa/entry permit fee (stay over 180 days) HKD 1,300
  - Source: https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/services/fees/visas/fees-for-application-for-visas-and-entry-permits.html
- Annual report appendix checkpoint
  - Student visas/entry permits issued in 2024: 74,466
  - Entrants admitted under IANG in 2024: 10,796
  - Source: https://www.immd.gov.hk/eng/publications/a_report_2024/appendix5.html

## Singapore

- Employment Pass eligibility floor
  - From 2025-01-01 (new applications): SGD 5,600 (financial services SGD 6,200), with higher bands by age
  - Same criteria apply to renewals from 2026-01-01
  - Source: https://www.mom.gov.sg/passes-and-permits/employment-pass/eligibility
- Student's Pass issuance fee SGD 60; multiple-journey visa fee SGD 30 (if applicable)
  - Source: https://www.ica.gov.sg/reside/STP/collect
- Work while studying in Singapore
  - Full-time students at approved institutions may work up to 16 hours/week during term; no hour limit during vacation
  - Source: https://www.mom.gov.sg/passes-and-permits/work-pass-exemption-for-foreign-students
